About Us

KAPI Limited manufactures and distributes insecticides for use in the home, for personnal use, for agricultural application, and for public use. We are the largest formulator of natural pyrethrum products in East Africa, and we produce more mosquito coils than any other company in Sub-Saharan Africa. Our products are very well known in Kenya, and East Africa and they include the Flower Range of mosquito control products as well as the Dudu-Krin range of insecticidal pet shampoos. Our flower and Combat Mosquito coils arr the coils of choice in Kenya. Our broad range of home use products and pet shampoos are sold in all key chain supermarkets and all quality independent supermarkets in Kenya. Our products are sold in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda and Rwanda. We also export to Nortern Europe, Southern Europe and Central America.

We were founded in 1964

We are based in Nakuru in the Beautiful Rift Valley. Our factory employs upto 150 Kenyans and we delight in manufacuting high quality insecticides for the Kenyan and East African Market as well as for global customers.
